A Letter For You

written with quiet thoughts and late night memories

Dear Lyanne Lei ng Mundo,

This is just something simple I wanted to make for you. Not dramatic. Not overdone. Just honest thoughts written quietly, like scenes in a slow movie.

I always think about how you love watching movies how you get lost in stories how you notice the small details most people miss. That kind of perspective is rare.

And of course, your love for F1. The speed, the focus, the discipline. It somehow fits your personality calm on the outside, intense when it matters.

You have such cool taste in music. The kind that feels like night drives city lights windows down thoughts running wild.

I also remember how you hate pink but love maroon, red, and black. Strong colors. Bold. Just like you.

This letter isn’t meant to be heavy. Just a reminder that someone out here appreciates you exactly the way you are.

— Krystian
